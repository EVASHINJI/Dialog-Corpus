# OpenSubtitles

**类型：**多轮、多语言、电影字幕

**来源：**Internet Movie Database

**规模：**

2.6 billion sentences (17.2 billion tokens) distributed over 60 languages

**下载链接：**

http://opus.nlpl.eu/OpenSubtitles.php
